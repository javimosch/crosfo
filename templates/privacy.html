Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Crosfo collects the following information:

  • Nickname (for user identification)
  • Social media links (platform and URL)
  • Thumbs up interactions (to track engagement)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the cross-follow community features
  • Track engagement through thumbs up
  • Display your social links to community members
  • Calculate reciprocity metrics

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely in our database. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Update your social links

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
